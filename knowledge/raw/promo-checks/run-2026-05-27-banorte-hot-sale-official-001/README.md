## Verified facts

- Banorte's Hot Sale portal groups promotions into cards, ahorro/inversiones, PyME/empresas, creditos and seguros.
- The general visible event dates on most pages are 2026-05-25 to 2026-06-02. Credito de Nomina also displays a headline vigencia from 2026-05-22 to 2026-06-02 but its terms say eligible contracts are 2026-05-25 to 2026-06-02.
- Public, no-login sources did not expose a full Tarjeta de Credito Digital Banorte Hot Sale cashback table. The Hot Sale portal sends card users to Tarjeta Favorita, which is a personalized promotions surface.
- Several promotions are financial-product acquisition offers, not checkout discounts for ecommerce carts.

## Open gaps

- Tarjeta de Credito / Tarjeta de Debito Hot Sale terms require validation inside Tarjeta Favorita app/web or a public official PDF before using them in purchase-route calculations.

- No checkout, login, account, card, or personalized offer evidence was used.
