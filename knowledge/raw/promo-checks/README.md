# Raw Promotion Checks

Store raw or near-raw evidence for bank, wallet, coupon, MSI, and merchant-promotion checks.

Use this for promotion terms and route evidence that is not primarily a product price. If a promotion is evaluated through a specific product page or checkout, link both the promo-check folder and the relevant price-check folder from `source-index.md`.

Examples:

- official bank terms;
- merchant promotion pages;
- copied checkout notes with no personal data;
- payment-rail exclusions;
- registration, minimum-spend, cap, and date rules;
- secondary discovery notes clearly marked as secondary.

Do not store card numbers, account data, credentials, cookies, full addresses, order numbers, or personal customer identifiers.
