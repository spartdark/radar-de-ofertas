  - Up to 18 MSI at Sam's Club physical stores.

  - No Sam's-specific Amex cashback was validated from that page; app-targeted Amex Offers must be checked separately.
- Sam's Club INVEX / Cashi evidence showed:
  - 3.5% Cashi bonus at Sam's Club and Walmart in store, club and online.

## Practical Interpretation

- For Sam's Club Hot Sale online baskets around $5,000-$9,999, BBVA is the clearest bank route because it can combine MSI with statement bonus at lower thresholds than Banamex/HSBC.
- For baskets of $10,000+, Banamex 18 MSI becomes competitive if registration is complete and Sam's is confirmed as participating in checkout/terms.
- For baskets of $13,500+, HSBC can beat BBVA/Banamex only if the user is Premier/ONE or in the 30% invited segment and Sam's is a participating online merchant.
- For everyday Sam's purchases or small baskets, Sam's Club INVEX + Cashi is the best Sam's-native cashback-style route, especially if MSI is needed and the user accepts Cashi as the reward wallet.
- Amex at Sam's is mainly a physical-store MSI route unless a targeted Amex Offer appears in the user's app.
