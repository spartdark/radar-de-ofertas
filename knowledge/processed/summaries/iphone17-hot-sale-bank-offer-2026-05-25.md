## Guardrails

- Avoid Walmart Mexico and Mercado Libre as requested.
- Treat Promodescuentos as a discovery/reference source, not final TyC.
- Validate bank registration/activation before purchase.
- Confirm checkout payment mode before paying, because bank promotions can fail if the order is split, posted late, paid through a wallet, or charged outside the promo window.
- For Banorte's main tiered bonus, use TDC Digital Banorte at one payment in participating merchants. User-provided terms exclude MSI, PayPal, and Mercado Pago.
- For Costco TDC Banamex coupons, do not assume PayPal stacking. The observed `VS123` route is a direct Costco coupon that produced $23,399 one-payment total in checkout.
- Sam's Club Mexico evidence did not reveal a better iPhone route than Amazon/Banamex, Banorte eligible stores, or Costco coupon routes.
